About Us

About Us

JWang Properties is a boutique Realty with a personal touch. Through our innovative system and custom video production, JWang Properties advances REALTORS to be the Superstars to serve our clients, industry and communities. We offer one stop customized Strategies and Service Plans tailored to each client’s individual needs in both residential and commercial real estate. We are a strong team and our mission is to bring value and joy to others with our knowledge, creativity and expertise. As a broker, Jenny devotes herself to serve clients with her wells of knowledge and passion, in both residential and commercial real estate. Because of her in-depth relationships with the industry and dynamic networks, she offers enormous resources to support her agents and better serve her clients.

Licensed in 2002, Jenny joined RE/MAX Metro. As a sales associate, Jenny earned countless awards, such as: Top Producer, RE/MAX 20/20, and RE/MAX Hall of fame. Obtaining her broker license in November 2018, Jenny founded JWang Properties, a boutique realty with a personal touch. Along with JWang Media, a custom video production to promote the agents and the business, Jenny has established a company to fully realize her vision as an entrepreneur. Her mission is to lead her team to bring value and joy to others with innovation, creativity, and expertise through the journey shared with clients.

Jenny is a supporter of our communities. She is a member of the Residential Specialist and Women’s Council of REALTORS. She is the Past President of Women’s Council of REALTORS, Houston network 2016. Jenny served on the Homeowner’s Association of Southpoint Townhomes for 9 years. Jenny currently is an Ambassador of the Houston Ballet serving on the Ambassador Committee and is a lifetime member of the University of Houston Alumni Association.
